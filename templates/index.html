Welcome to the Couples Clicking Game!

About the Game

The Couples Clicking Game is a fun and competitive game where you and your significant other can compete to click a button the most times in a given time frame.

How to Play

Log in or create an account to start playing. Once you're logged in, you can start a new game session, click the button to increase your score, and see your game results and history.
